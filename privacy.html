Privacy Policy

Mehrle Holdings LLC · Last updated June 2026

Mehrle Holdings LLC ("we," "us," "our") builds and publishes software applications. We take privacy seriously: we collect only what a product needs to function, we do not sell personal information, and we do not use third-party advertising or tracking networks. This policy explains how privacy works across this website and our products.

This website

The website at mehrleholdingsllc.com is an informational site. We do not use cookies, advertising pixels, or third-party analytics trackers on it, and we do not collect personal information from visitors. Standard server logs kept by our hosting provider may temporarily record technical data such as IP address and browser type for security and reliability; these are not used to identify or profile you.

Each app has its own privacy policy. The data an app collects depends on what that app does. Our apps' policies are linked from their own pages — for example, Pour's policy lives at getpour.app/privacy.html.

Our approach across all products

  • Minimal collection. We collect only the information needed to provide the app's features.
  • No sale of data. We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information.
  • No third-party ad or tracking SDKs. Our apps do not include advertising networks or cross-app tracking.
  • Sign in with Apple. Where an account is needed, we use Sign in with Apple; you may choose to hide your email behind Apple's private relay.
  • On-device where possible. Sensitive operations — such as hashing contacts for friend-matching — happen on your device whenever we can do so.

Your choices

You can review and delete your data from within our apps where that feature is offered, revoke Sign in with Apple from your Apple ID settings, and contact us to request deletion of information associated with your account.

Children

Our products are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

We may update this policy as our products evolve. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
