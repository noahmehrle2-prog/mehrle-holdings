Pour

A drink journal that remembers the moment, not the rating.

Log a beer at a taproom, a glass of wine at dinner, or a morning coffee in two taps. Add a photo, a few notes, who you were with, and where you were — then watch your personal map and year-in-review fill in. No scores, no badges, no ads. The memory around the drink, not the score of the drink.
